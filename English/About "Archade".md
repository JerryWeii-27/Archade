# About "Archade"

[切换至中文 (Switch to Chinese)](../中文/关于“游戏图书馆”.md)

Welcome to Archade, a place where we celebrate the joy of simple, fun games that bring people together.

It all started with a sweet memory from my childhood. My grandma and I used to play [Mikado](Games/Mikado.md) together, when I was still in primary school. It is a game that needs nothing more than a bunch of popsicle sticks. It was simple but incredibly fun. 

Fast forward to my high school days, I brought Mikado to play with my roommates. We usually spent hours glued to our screens playing video games, but to our surprise, we ended up playing Mikado for hours and having a blast. It was refreshing to see how much joy a simple game could bring us, away from the toxic addictiveness of video games. We found ourselves less frustrated and more relaxed.

This inspired me to create Archade. I wanted to share the magic of these simple games with everyone. Here, you’ll find a collection of games that are easy to learn, need minimal stuff, and are super entertaining. Whether you’re looking to relive childhood memories, spend quality time with friends and family, or just take a break from screens, we’ve got you covered.

So, dive in, explore our games, and let’s bring back the fun of playing together. Welcome to Archade!
